Website Of Krisztián Hofstädter

Hey there! I’m a Hungarian interdisciplinary researcher living in England. My main focus has been on using rhythm to induce and maintain altered states of consciousness, but I am also interested in many other things, for instance in developing my personal knowledge management, and keeping myself and my family healthy.


Now

I work on the Enhancing Audio Description II project as a postdoc at the University of York, teach the Navigating the Digital World module as a part-time lecturer at the University of Essex and develop chapters of my doctoral thesis into papers for journals as an independent researcher. I am also personalizing the zettlekasten method in VSCodium using Foam and redesigning my website to better archive my work.


Some Past Works

Most of my music can be found on Bandcamp, some videos on YouTube and academic publications on ResearchGate.
